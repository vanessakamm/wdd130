Our best trips !

White water splash (3 day trip)
Prepare for the ultimate rush as you embark on our White Water Rush trip! Get ready to paddle through intense rapids, as the river takes you on an exciting ride. Feel the spray of the water on your face as you navigate the roaring waves, surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. Our expert guides will ensure your safety while providing an exhilarating and unforgettable rafting experience. Get your heart racing with this thrilling adventure!
Wild river experience (1 day trip)
Embark the Wild River Expedition and immerse yourself in nature. This 3 day trip will take you deep into a remote wilderness, where you'll navigate a wild river surrounded by untouched landscapes. Encounter Class III and IV rapids, camp under starry skies, and witness the wonders of wildlife in their natural habitat. Led by experienced guides, this expedition promises an unforgettable journey through pristine wilderness.

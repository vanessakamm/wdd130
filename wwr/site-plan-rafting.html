Overview

Purpose

First of all we aim to increase our visibility becuase our company provides fun water rafting experiences. We know this will attract more customers who are searching for a similar experience. Second, in a day and age where technology dominates, we want to make reservations and booking more efficient.Customers would be able to explore our website and find what they are looking for.Lastly, with the creation of this website we wish to connect with our customers and build a good image that revolves around family and friends.

Audience

Water Rafting is a water sport that is suitable for people of all ages. Especially for people who seek a challenge or adventure and would like to share this experience. What better than sharing these experiences with family and friends.If you enjoy splashes and challeneges, water rafting is for you!
